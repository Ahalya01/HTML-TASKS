Welcome to My Website

Welcome to JNTUH University College of Engineering Manthani

The University College of Engineering, Manthani as a part of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad is committed to the ongoing creation of a vital engineering community animated by a love of learning along with academic excellence and the pursuit of truth. The College strives to foster an environment based on open enquiry and service to the larger community. This mission is undertaken under the aegis of the university with an aim to impart quality, value-based education in various fields of technology

My 5 Favorite Things About JNTUHCEM

JNTUHCEM is renowned for its highly qualified and experienced faculty who are dedicated to student success. The college offers state-of-the-art infrastructure, including advanced laboratories, a comprehensive library, and well-equipped classrooms, providing an excellent learning environment. Students are encouraged to engage in innovative research, with access to cutting-edge technology and strong support for their projects. The vibrant campus life includes various cultural, technical, and sports events, allowing students to develop leadership and social skills. Additionally, the strong alumni network provides mentorship and career support, helping students connect with industry professionals and successfully transition into their careers.
